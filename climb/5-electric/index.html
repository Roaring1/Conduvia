    Tier 5 — Electric¶

    The chemistry gateway. An AC grid powers electrolysis: aluminum, chlor-alkali, ferroalloys, metallurgical silicon.

    5
    Tier 5 — Electric
    Unlocks after: Steel and a DC dynamo.→Leads to: AC grid, electrolysis, arc furnace — the chemistry gateway.

    17 quests · 15 on the win-path · 1 branches

    Briefing

    AC Grids and Early Automation¶

    Tier 4 is the "site becomes a power system"¶

    Steel/DC got you: heavy plant, dynamos, local DC networks, and motors. Now you add what makes power go far and scale:

    • AC generation and switching practice
    • transformers (step up / step down)
    • three-phase transmission for distance + motors
    • rectification back to DC for old loads and heavy processes
    • early electronics (vacuum tubes) as the bridge into instrumentation + control
    • plant-wide meters, relays, alarms
    • early regulation and automatic control
    • a central control room that makes the plant readable
    Win-path

    Chlor-Alkali and the Chemical Gateway¶

    Unlocks after: AC In, Heavy DC Out

    Electrolysis opens the chemical frontier¶

    Your rectifier bay now outputs heavy DC. The next step: using that current to split matter into things that don't occur naturally in pure form.

    • Chlor-alkali cell (DiAPHRAGM or membrane): NaCl brine + DC → Cl2 + NaOH + H2
    • Downs cell (fused-salt): molten NaCl + DC → Na metal + Cl2
    • Müller-Kühne HF: CaF2 (fluorite) + H2SO4 → HF + CaSO4

    These are the three master reagents for all Tier 4+ chemistry: Cl2 for chlorination, NaOH for leaching/digestion, HF for niobium/zirconium/fluorite processing.

    Do this: - retrieve Process brine solution through the chlor-alkali cell.
    yields: BSbrine solution×8 - retrieve Collect chlorine gas and sodium hydroxide (caustic soda) from the cell outputs.
    yields: CGchlorine gas×4 SHsodium hydroxide×4 - retrieve Produce hydrofluoric acid from fluorite + sulfuric acid.
    yields: HAhydrofluoric acid×2 - retrieve Produce sodium metal via the Downs cell (fused-salt electrolysis of NaCl).
    yields: SMsodium metal×2

    Rewards: CGchlorine gas×4 SHsodium hydroxide×4

    Arc Furnace Chemistry¶

    Unlocks after: Chlor-Alkali and the Chemical Gateway

    Electric arc furnace chemistry chain¶

    The electric arc furnace does more than make steel: its extreme temperatures drive carbothermic and thermite reactions conventional furnaces cannot reach.

    • MG-Si (98% silicon): SiO2 + 2C → Si + 2CO at ~2000°C -- gateway to semiconductor silicon
    • Ferrochrome: chromite + coke → FeCr -- essential for stainless and superalloy grades
    • Ferronickel: pentlandite + coke + limestone → FeNi -- nickel source for stainless, Inconel
    • Stainless steel 18-8: Fe + FeCr + FeNi in argon-oxygen decarburization converter

    Do this: - retrieve Produce metallurgical-grade silicon (98% pure) by carbothermic reduction of silica.
    yields: MGmetallurgical-grade silicon×4 - retrieve Smelt ferrochrome (from chromite + coke) and ferronickel (from pentlandite + coke).
    yields: FEferrochrome×2 FEferronickel×2 - retrieve Smelt a stainless steel 18-8 ingot from the ferroalloys in an AOD converter.
    yields: SSstainless steel ingot×1

    Rewards: SSstainless steel sheet×4 MGmetallurgical-grade silicon×2

    Hall-Heroult Aluminium¶

    Unlocks after: Chlor-Alkali and the Chemical Gateway

    Smelt aluminium from bauxite¶

    Aluminium is the most abundant structural metal on Earth but exists only as oxides locked in bauxite. Two electrochemical steps liberate it:

    1. Bayer process: NaOH dissolves bauxite → aluminium hydroxide → filter → calcine → Al2O3
    2. Hall-Heroult cell: Al2O3 dissolved in molten cryolite + DC + carbon anodes → liquid Al + CO2

    Result: one of the lightest, most corrosion-proof structural metals. Essential for aircraft, vehicles, overhead power cables, and all future rocketry.

    Do this: - retrieve Have cryolite bath salt and baked carbon anodes ready for the reduction pot.
    yields: CRcryolite×2 CAcarbon anode block×4 - retrieve Produce aluminium ingots via Hall-Heroult electrolysis.
    yields: AIaluminum ingot×8 - retrieve Roll sheet and produce an aerospace-grade Al alloy for structures.
    yields: ASaluminum sheet×4 AAaerospace aluminum alloy×2

    Rewards: ASaluminum sheet×4 AAaerospace aluminum alloy×1

    Electrorefining and Stainless Steel¶

    Unlocks after: Arc Furnace Chemistry

    Purify metals to industry-grade purity¶

    Copper electrorefining: blister copper (~98.5%) cast as anodes, dissolved in CuSO4/H2SO4, and re-deposited onto stainless cathodes → 99.99% Cu. Precious metals (Ag, Au, Se) fall as valuable anode slime.

    Nickel electrorefining (Sherritt Gordon process): pentlandite concentrate → ammoniacal leach → purification → electrodeposition → 99.9% Ni cathode.

    Nickel cathode is the input for all downstream superalloy VIM melts: stainless, Inconel.

    Do this: - retrieve Provide blister copper anodes for the electrorefining bath.
    yields: BCblister copper anode×4 - retrieve Process nickel concentrate through the Sherritt Gordon leach-electro route.
    yields: NCnickel concentrate×4 NCnickel cathode×4 - retrieve Roll stainless steel sheet for fabrication stock.
    yields: SSstainless steel sheet×4

    Rewards: NCnickel cathode×2 SSstainless steel sheet×2
