    Tokens & Rewards¶

    As you finish quests in a chapter you earn category tokens — think of them as proof you’ve mastered that era’s skills. Spend them here: upgrade three lower tokens into one higher token, or cash tokens in for useful bundles. The exchange is always open.

    Briefing

    Upgrade Tokens¶

    Upgrade Tokens¶

    Exchange 3 tokens of one tier for 1 token of the next. All four categories can be upgraded independently.

    Briefing

    Survival Rewards¶

    Survival Rewards¶

    Spend Survival tokens for material bundles that give you a head-start on staying alive. Costs are in Survival tokens of the appropriate tier.

    Briefing

    Tech Rewards¶

    Tech Rewards¶

    Spend Tech tokens for tool and equipment bundles. Saves you some of the more tedious crafting steps.

    Briefing

    Industry Rewards¶

    Industry Rewards¶

    Spend Industry tokens for fuel, materials, and processing bundles. Helps keep the production line moving when a bottleneck hits.

    Win-path

    Survival I → II¶

    Survival I → II¶

    Exchange 3× Survival Token I for 1× Survival Token II.

    Useful when you have a surplus of early-era tokens.

    Do this: - retrieve Have 3 Survival Token I ready for exchange.
    yields: STSurvival Token I×3

    Costs: STSurvival Token I×3

    Rewards: STSurvival Token II×1
