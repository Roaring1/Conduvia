# Your first hour

You wake up with nothing. Here’s how to not die, in order. Every step here is the
opening of the [Survival chapter](../climb/0-survival.md) — open your Quest Log
(++j++) and it’ll track each one for you.

## 1. Get cordage

Everything early hangs together with rope. Gather **plant fibers** from the
ground and brush, then craft :ci[rope]. Cordage is the first “technology” humans
ever made — you can’t haft a tool or build a shelter without it.

## 2. Make a cutting edge

Scavenge stone and knap a sharp edge — a :ci[stone_knife_flake] or hand axe. Now you
can cut, scrape, and process everything else.

## 3. Find water

Dehydration is a clock. Locate a clean water source early and drink. Keep an eye
on your needs while you work.

## 4. Light a fire

Fire is heat, safety, and cooking. Build a campfire and get it lit. This is also
your first taste of **controlled heat** — the thing the entire tech tree is
secretly about.

## 5. Set up to sleep

Place a **bedroll** to claim your spawn point, so a bad night doesn’t send you
back to square one. Then survive until morning.

!!! tip "You’ve hit the first checkpoint"
    Water, fire, a blade, and a safe night — that’s Survival I complete. From
    here the game opens up: trapping, fishing, your first cooked meal, simple
    storage, and the first farm plots are all **optional branches** you can take
    in any order before you start the [Stone](../climb/1-stone.md) climb.
