# Tokens & Rewards

As you finish quests in a chapter you earn **category tokens** — think of them as proof you’ve mastered that era’s skills. Spend them here: **upgrade** three lower tokens into one higher token, or cash tokens in for useful **bundles**. The exchange is always open.
